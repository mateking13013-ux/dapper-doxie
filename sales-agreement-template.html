Health and Wellness Guarantee - Dapper Doxie

We raise our Dachshund puppies in-home with proactive veterinary care. The following protections apply to every puppy placed.

1. Veterinary Examination

The Buyer must take their Dachshund puppy to a licensed veterinarian for a full health check within seven (7) days of receiving the puppy. If a serious congenital defect or underlying illness is found, Dapper Doxie will issue a full refund of the adoption fee, including transportation costs, upon safe return of the puppy.

2. Breed-Specific Coverage

Because Dachshunds can be prone to specific genetic conditions, Dapper Doxie provides a lifetime guarantee against serious congenital defects. If diagnosed by two (2) licensed veterinarians, the Buyer may return the puppy for either a full refund of the adoption fee (including transportation costs) or a replacement puppy of equal value.

3. Communicable Diseases

If the puppy is diagnosed with a communicable disease within the first fourteen (14) days of arrival, Dapper Doxie will refund the full adoption fee, including transportation costs, upon the puppy's return.

4. Transportation and Delivery

Shipping: If the puppy is shipped, the Buyer is responsible for transportation expenses. Dapper Doxie uses trusted pet transport services and provides tracking details for peace of mind.

Pickup: If the Buyer chooses in-person pickup, all remaining balance must be paid in full before or at pickup.

5. Cost and Payment

The full adoption fee must be paid prior to delivery or pickup. Any unpaid balance will delay release of the puppy until payment is completed.

6. Buyer's Responsibilities

The Buyer agrees to provide lifelong care, including nutritious food, fresh water, safe shelter, daily exercise, and regular veterinary visits. The Buyer must also comply with all local and state regulations regarding dog ownership.

This agreement represents the full understanding between Dapper Doxie and the Buyer. Any changes must be made in writing and signed by both parties.
